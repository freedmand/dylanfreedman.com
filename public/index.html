Ripple Plastic

Ripple Plastic is a virtual reality experience I created in conjunction with award-winning photographer Mandy Barker and the Stanford Journalism Program. The interactive exploration highlights the growing plastic pollution in the worldâ€™s oceans by juxtaposing plastic debris Barker photographed on beaches with a guided narration.

I coded the experience using the A-Frame Web.VR framework (source code).
